About Us

ExpenseTracker is built for students who want a simple way to monitor spending and keep financial habits under control. Log your daily expenses, group them into categories, and set budgets to see exactly where your money goes.

The app features a clean, responsive design with reports, profile management, and a quick way to share feedback.
